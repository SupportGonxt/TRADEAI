**Gap:** No auto-remediation

**Gap:** Slow approval cycle (days vs. hours)  
**Impact:** Missed opportunities

#### 5. Data Entry
**Current:** Manual forms  
**Best-in-Class:** Auto-populate + validation

**Impact:** Errors, slow processing
